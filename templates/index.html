Real Estate Data Analytics

Understanding Real Estate Markets through Data Analytics.

This presentation analyzes the impact of economic factors on the real estate markets and also builds a model for predicting real estate markets in the future based on these economic indicators.

The goal of our project is to predict the strength of the US real estate market based on certain economic and demographic indicators.
We will perform a historical analysis of the real estate market and the level of the various economic and demographic indicators over time.
We then will use this historical data with machine learning to set up a model that will predict the level of future real estate markets based on changes in the various economic and demographic factors.
For example, when unemployment is low and interest rates are low, we expect to see many buyers in the market purchasing homes.
